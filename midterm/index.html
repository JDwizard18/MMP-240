Star Wars Visons

September 22, 2021, 2 Seasons

Star Wars: Visions is an animated anthology series of non-canon short films created for the streaming service, Disney+. It's initial release was comprised of nine original short films from various well known Japanese animation studios that present a different cultural perspective of Star Wars and it is all done in the various anime styles that each studio is known for.

On May 29, 2022, a 2nd volume of Star Wars: Visions was announced with a focus on showcasing animation studios from around the world featuring a wide variety of animation styles and cultural references. The 2nd volume debuted on May 4, 2023 on Disney+ with the same accompanying featurette as the first volume, and proved to be similar success.

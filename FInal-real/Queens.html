Fine Arts

Degree: A.S

"The Fine Arts Program, coordinated by the Humanities Department, leads to an Associate in Science (AS) Degree. Degree: A.S

The A.S. degree program in Digital Art and Design prepares students to enter the greatly expanding field of new media.

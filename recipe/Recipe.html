Double Chocolate Chip Frappuccino copycat

One sip of this creamy, chocolatey, homemade treat wil makes youfeel like your having one from Starbucks! While many will argue that nothing beats the original, let’s be honest – Starbucks isn’t known for It’s budget-friendly options. With this copycat recipe, you get to enjoy one of best beverages at a fraction of the cost. There’s no need to wait in line, either! It’s also a breeze to make. You don’t need to be an expert barista to pull it off. Just throw in all the ingredients in a blender and blend away!

recipe

  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/3 cup chocolate chips
  • 3 tablespoons chocolate syrup
  • 2 cups ice
  • 1/8 teaspoon vanilla

steps

  1. Add the milk, sugar, chocolate chips, chocolate syrup, ice, and vanilla to a blender. Blend until smooth and creamy.
  2. Pour it in a glass. Enjoy!

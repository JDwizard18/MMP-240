CUNY TV Wins 21st New York Emmy Award For Animated Opening Sequence Of 'Shades Of U.S'

April 27, 2020

CUNY TV Art Director Adam Finger prevailed in the category of Graphic Arts: Animation for his soulful opening sequence of “Shades of U.S.,” a series that explores the complexities of people’s lives through the prisms of color, race, religion and sexual orientation. Finger’s 30-second animated show opening shines a spotlight on society’s unsung ordinary heroes through statue-like images of culturally diverse individuals engaged in everyday rituals.
